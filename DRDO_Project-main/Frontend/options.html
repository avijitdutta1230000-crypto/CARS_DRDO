CARS

The Contract for Acquisition of Research Services (CARS) is a DRDO initiative designed to foster research collaborations with academic and research institutions. Under the CARS scheme, DRDO sponsors research projects that align with its strategic objectives, enabling institutions to contribute to defense research and development.

CAPSI

The Contract for Acquisition of Professional Services through IDST (CAPSI) is a strategic framework established by the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) to engage external entities in defense-related projects. This initiative facilitates collaboration with private industries, and research organizations to undertake projects that contribute to the development of advanced defense technologies. CAPSI aims to leverage external expertise and innovation to accelerate the research and development process, ensuring that the Indian defense sector remains at the forefront of technological advancements.
