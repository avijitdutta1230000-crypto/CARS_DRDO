Key Highlights

The CARS (Contract for Acquisition of Research Services) Portal is a dedicated digital platform developed for the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) to facilitate and streamline the entire lifecycle of research service contracts. Designed with a focus on transparency, efficiency, and security, the portal serves as a one-stop solution for managing proposals, contract approvals, fund disbursements, progress tracking, and reporting. It enables seamless interaction between researchers, institutions, and DRDO by automating critical workflows, ensuring compliance, and providing real-time visibility into project milestones and resource utilization. With role-based dashboards, secure document management, and audit-ready reporting tools, the CARS Portal empowers DRDO to accelerate innovation while maintaining full oversight and governance of research engagements.
